## Key Rules & FAQ

- **Team size:** 2–4 members. Solo participants will be helped to find teammates.
- **Free to attend:** No participation fee. Food & accommodation included.
- **In-person only:** No remote participation.
- **Project start:** Hacking begins only after the opening ceremony. Earlier commits = disqualification.
- **Stay mandatory:** Once checked in, participants must remain on campus until closing (Jun 14).
- **Open innovation:** Projects don't need to follow a specific theme — build what matters.
- **Travel:** No travel reimbursement provided.
- **Food & stay:** Meals + gender-separated sleeping areas arranged on campus.
- **Code of Conduct:** [HackPrix CoC](https://hackprix.tech/coc) + [MLH CoC](https://github.com/MLH/mlh-policies/blob/main/code-of-conduct.md)
